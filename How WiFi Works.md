# 📶 How WiFi Works

## 1. Introduction

WiFi is a wireless technology that allows devices to connect to the internet without using cables.

It works using **radio waves** to transmit data between devices and a router.

---

## 2. Main Components of WiFi

| Component | Function |
|-----------|---------|
| Router | Connects to internet and broadcasts WiFi signal |
| Device | Phone, laptop, or tablet receiving WiFi |
| Radio Waves | Used to transmit data wirelessly |

---

## 3. How WiFi Works

### Step 1: Internet Comes to Router

Your internet service provider sends internet data to your **router**.

---

### Step 2: Router Sends Wireless Signals

The router converts the internet data into **radio signals** and broadcasts them.

---

### Step 3: Device Connects to Router

Your phone or laptop receives the WiFi signals and connects to the router.

---

### Step 4: Data Exchange

Data travels back and forth between your device and the router.

Example:

Device ↔ Router ↔ Internet


---

## 4. Real-Life Example

When you open **Instagram** using WiFi:

1. Your phone sends a request to the router
2. The router forwards the request to the internet
3. The server sends data back
4. The data reaches your phone through the router

---

## 5. Simple Summary

WiFi allows devices to connect to the internet wirelessly by using radio waves to communicate with a router.
